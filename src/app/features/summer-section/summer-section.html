Customer Experience (CX) Team

This summer, I worked within the Customer Experience organization, specifically with the Customer Portal team. This team is responsible for a major feature that enables two-way communication between our internal teams Sales and Construction and our customers.

The core functionality of the portal allows the Construction team to provide bi-weekly updates aligned with the project timeline of each home. This ensures that all stakeholders such as Sales, Construction, and the customer are aligned on the progress and upcoming milestones of the homebuilding process.

The Customer Portal team is a cross-functional group composed of a Senior Manager, Business Analyst, Product Manager, Scrum Master, Developers, and QA professionals. We collaborated closely throughout each sprint to deliver incremental improvements and ensure a seamless user experience.
