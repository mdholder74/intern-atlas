This summer, I had the opportunity to work with the Customer Experience product team, specifically within the Customer Portal Vertical. This team is responsible for the Customer Portal used by homebuyers, focusing on delivering features such as service requests, resources, messaging, and home updates.

The Customer Portal Vertical is a cross-functional group made up of an IT Director, IT Senior Manager, Product Owner, Scrum Master, Analysts, Developers, and QA professionals . We collaborated closely throughout each sprint to deliver incremental improvements to the platform.

During my time with the team, we focused on a major feature called two-way communication, which enhances communication between our Sales, Construction, and Homebuyers. This functionality allows homebuyers to respond directly to updates and messages from the sales or construction teams. It enables seamless two-way communication, which is essential for delivering a great customer experience and keeping everyone informed.
